House of Representatives Preference Flows

This visualisation shows preference flows between candidates at the 2025 Australian federal election. Each round, the candidate with the fewest votes is elminiated, and their votes are distributed to the remaining candidates based on voter preferences. This visualisation shows the flow of these preferences between rounds. The final two candidates are always placed on the top and bottom of the graph, respectively.

Voting data is sourced from the Australian Electoral Commission.
